In-Depth Family and Personal Histories

Larger Size Project

Long form books can take several months to more than year to complete, but they are worth the time and investment. These complex histories trace a family’s roots and give current and future generations a detailed portrait of decades and generations past. They tell the story of lives worth remembering.

Reminiscence Books voices

Reminiscence Books

Medium Size Project

Our signature product is the mid-length reminiscence book for downsizers. It is geared towards those who are letting go of a beloved home or some of their belongings. Our interview team comes to your home to create a photographic record of cherished items and to record the stories behind each one, which are then combined into a beautifully designed book.

Voice Recordings a la carte

Voice Recordings

Smaller Size Project

Preserve the voice of your loved one forever. Our expert interviewer comes to your home and draws out the stories that make up a life. These are then lightly edited and preserved on private online storage and on a USB card in an attractive presentation box.

Why create your personal history?

Why Create Your Personal History?

Have you been meaning to write down your family history or personal story? Is it time to capture an elder’s stories before it is too late? These stories are important, whether your fame extends around the block or across the world. A professionally produced personal history brings the past to life and preserves the vitality of family stories for generations to come. We’re here to help. Call or e-mail for a free consultation.

How we work with you

How We Work With You

Through interviews and photographs, we create legacy books and recordings for you. Our husband and wife team brings a wealth of project experience and care to every book and recording we create. We provide design, writing, editing, photography and photo restoration, genealogy and historical research services as needed to create a legacy that you can keep and share. We start with a free consultation to understand what your goals are. We follow with a proposal and then we work with you to produce a book or recording that ensures future generations will hear the stories that you care about.

About Miles and Leora

Miles Hochstein

Miles brings his passion for genealogy and history, and an extensive background in information technology, to his work for Personal History Design. His eye for accuracy and strong sense of design insure that the books and recordings we produce are true works of art.

Leora Troper

Leora brings her skills as research librarian and a writer to create personal memoirs and histories for our clients. She ensures that clients have a life story that is interesting to read and backed up by a solid historical setting. She brings a strong aesthetic sense to the final look of all of Personal History Design’s products.
